Alpha Team's project 2: Musical Hit Finder

This dashboard compares the properties of music, according to Spotify's classification of four features: Valence, Energy, Danceability and Popularity.

In By Country, you will find a chart with a benchmark of Produced vs Consumed music features in 8 countries from various continents.

By Genre displays a hexbin selection pannel that will let you choose between genres to check their features in a radar chart.

Please take a look at the Git repo to check the details on data sources.
